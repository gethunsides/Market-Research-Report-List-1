<p><p>The Reverse Osmosis Film market is primarily divided into two types: Cellulose Based and Thin Film Composite membranes. Cellulose Based membranes, made from natural cellulose materials, offer moderate performance and are cost-effective for simpler water purification needs. In contrast, Thin Film Composite membranes utilize advanced polymer materials, providing superior filtration efficiency, higher flux rates, and greater chemical resistance, making them ideal for more demanding applications in industries like desalination and wastewater treatment. Each type serves distinct market needs.</p></p>
