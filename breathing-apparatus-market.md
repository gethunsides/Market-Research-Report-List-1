<p><strong>The Breathing Apparatus Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Circuit</li><li>Closed-Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The breathing apparatus market is primarily divided into two types: open-circuit and closed-circuit systems. Open-circuit systems expel exhaled air into the environment, commonly found in applications like firefighting or diving. In contrast, closed-circuit systems recycle exhaled air, removing carbon dioxide and reintroducing oxygen, making them suitable for longer-duration tasks in confined spaces. These differing technologies cater to specific operational needs, enhancing safety and efficiency in various industries, including emergency response, industrial work, and military applications.</p></p>
