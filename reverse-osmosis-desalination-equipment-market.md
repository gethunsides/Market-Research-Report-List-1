<p><p>Reverse Osmosis Desalination Equipment is a technology used to remove salts and impurities from seawater or brackish water, making it suitable for human consumption and irrigation. The growing demand for freshwater resources due to population growth, urbanization, and industrialization has significantly boosted the need for desalination technologies.</p><p>The Reverse Osmosis Desalination Equipment Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.6% during the forecast period. Emerging economies are increasingly investing in infrastructure and initiatives to improve water accessibility, further propelling the market.
