<p><p>The thermal silica market encompasses three primary types: thermal grease, thermal paste, and thermal patches, each designed for effective heat dissipation in electronic components. Thermal grease is a viscous substance used to enhance thermal conductivity between surfaces. Thermal paste, often more engineered, provides superior thermal transfer and is commonly used in CPUs and GPUs. Thermal patches, typically pre-cut adhesive pads, offer a convenient solution for direct attachment, ensuring efficient heat transfer without mess, making them ideal for various applications.</p></p>
