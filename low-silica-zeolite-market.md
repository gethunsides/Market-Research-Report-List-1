<p><p>The low silica zeolite market comprises two main types: natural and synthetic zeolites. Natural zeolites are mined from volcanic ash deposits, known for their unique porous structures and various industrial applications, including agriculture and water treatment. In contrast, synthetic zeolites are artificially manufactured, allowing for tailored properties and consistency, often used in catalysis, gas separation, and detergents. Both types serve diverse sectors, benefiting from low silica content, which enhances their performance in specific applications.</p></p>
