<p><ul><li>Modular Softwall Cleanrooms</li><li>Modular Hardwall Cleanrooms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The modular cleanroom market comprises two main types: modular softwall cleanrooms and modular hardwall cleanrooms. Modular softwall cleanrooms feature flexible fabric walls, allowing for easy customization and reconfiguration to accommodate various processes, making them ideal for low to medium contamination control needs. In contrast, modular hardwall cleanrooms are constructed with rigid panels, providing enhanced structural integrity and a higher level of contamination control. These are suitable for industries requiring stringent cleanroom standards, such as pharmaceuticals and biotechnology.</p></p>
