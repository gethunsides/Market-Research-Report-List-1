<p><strong>The VOC Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC analyzer market primarily comprises two key types: PID (Photoionization Detector) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) analyzers. PID analyzers utilize ultraviolet light to ionize volatile organic compounds, offering high sensitivity and quick response times, making them ideal for detecting low concentrations of VOCs in various environments. In contrast, MOS analyzers rely on semiconductor materials that change resistivity in the presence of VOCs, providing cost-effective solutions for general monitoring applications, though they may have lower specificity and sensitivity compared to PID devices.</p></p>
