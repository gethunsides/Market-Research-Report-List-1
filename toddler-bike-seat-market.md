<p><p>The toddler bike seat market is primarily divided into front-mounted and rear-mounted options. Front-mounted seats attach to the bike's frame, allowing parents to closely monitor their child while riding, fostering interaction. They provide a lower center of gravity, enhancing bike stability. In contrast, rear-mounted seats are fitted on the rear rack or frame, offering more space for the child and accommodating a wider range of bike types. This setup often provides a more comfortable ride for older toddlers while maintaining safety.</p></p>
