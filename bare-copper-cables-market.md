<p><p>The Bare Copper Cables market consists of three primary types based on their draw characteristics: Soft Drawn, Medium Hard Drawn, and Hard Drawn. Soft Drawn cables are highly flexible and suitable for applications requiring ease of bending. Medium Hard Drawn cables offer a balance of flexibility and strength, making them ideal for moderate-load environments. Hard Drawn cables are rigid and designed for high tension applications, providing superior conductivity and durability but with limited flexibility, typically used in construction and heavy-duty electrical applications.</p></p>
