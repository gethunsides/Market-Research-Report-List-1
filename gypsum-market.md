<p><p>The gypsum market encompasses various types, each serving distinct purposes. **Plasterboard**, widely used in construction, offers fire resistance and soundproofing for walls and ceilings. **Plaster blocks** are pre-cast gypsum units used for partitions and walls, providing durability and ease of installation. **Gypsum fibreboard** combines gypsum with cellulose fibers, enhancing strength and moisture resistance, ideal for flooring and wall systems. **Decorative plaster** is used for aesthetic finishes, creating textured or smooth surfaces in interiors. **Others** include specialized gypsum products like molds, casts, and additives for niche applications.</p></p>
