<p><strong>The Photomask Inspection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photomask Inspection System market features two primary methods: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB). The DD method involves comparing an inspected die against a previously inspected die to identify defects, focusing on uniformity and consistency throughout production. In contrast, the DB method compares the inspected die to a comprehensive database of known good dies, allowing for the detection of deviations from established design specifications. Both methods are essential for ensuring the quality and reliability of photomasks in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
