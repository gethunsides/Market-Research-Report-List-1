<p><ul><li>Primary Fatty Amine</li><li>Secondary Fatty Amine</li><li>Tertiary Fatty Amine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fatty Amine market is categorized into Primary, Secondary, and Tertiary Fatty Amines based on their chemical structure. Primary Fatty Amines have one alkyl group attached to the nitrogen atom, making them reactive and widely used in surfactants and agrochemicals. Secondary Fatty Amines have two alkyl groups, offering moderate reactivity and applications in corrosion inhibitors and emulsifiers. Tertiary Fatty Amines feature three alkyl groups, providing stability and versatility in quaternary ammonium compounds and biocides. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
