<p><p>The Transmission Electron Microscope (TEM) market consists of various types, including Conventional TEM, Low-Voltage Electron Microscopes, and Cryo-microscopy. Conventional TEM offers high-resolution imaging for materials and biological samples but requires significant sample preparation. Low-Voltage Electron Microscopes operate at lower voltages, reducing sample damage and providing imaging capabilities for sensitive materials. Cryo-microscopy preserves samples in a frozen state, allowing researchers to study biological specimens in their natural environments, making it crucial for understanding cellular structures and processes.</p></p>
