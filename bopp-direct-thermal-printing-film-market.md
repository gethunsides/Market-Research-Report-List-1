<p><p>BOPP Direct Thermal Printing Film is categorized into two primary types: White Thermal Printing Film and Transparent Thermal Printing Film. The White Thermal Printing Film is opaque, ideal for creating vivid labels and graphics that require strong contrast for readability. In contrast, Transparent Thermal Printing Film offers a clear substrate, making it suitable for applications where visibility of the underlying surface is desired, such as product packaging or labeling. Both types cater to various industries, enhancing branding and information display.</p></p>
