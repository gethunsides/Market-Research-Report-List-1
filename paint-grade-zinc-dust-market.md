<p><strong>The Paint Grade Zinc Dust Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Atomization Method</li><li>Hydraulic Atomization Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paint grade zinc dust market can be segmented based on production methods: the air atomization method and the hydraulic atomization method. </p><p>In the air atomization method, molten zinc is dispersed into fine particles using high-pressure air, resulting in a high purity and uniform particle size, ideal for coatings. In contrast, the hydraulic atomization method employs high-pressure water streams to produce zinc dust, leading to larger particle sizes and varying shapes, which may impact the performance of the final paint products.</p></p>
