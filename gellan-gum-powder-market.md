<p><p>Gellan gum powder is a versatile hydrocolloid used as a gelling, thickening, and stabilizing agent in food, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics. It is categorized into two types: Low Acyl Gellan Gum and High Acyl Gellan Gum. Low Acyl Gellan Gum forms firm, brittle gels and is ideal for applications requiring high clarity and stability, such as desserts and jellies. High Acyl Gellan Gum produces soft, elastic gels, suitable for creamy textures in dairy products and sauces. Both types cater to diverse industry needs based on their unique gel properties.</p></p>
