<p><p>Ball mill liners serve as protective barriers and influence the grinding efficiency of the mill. The two primary types are rubber mill liners and metal mill liners. Rubber liners are lightweight, offer noise reduction, and provide excellent wear resistance, making them suitable for a variety of applications. In contrast, metal liners, typically made from steel or alloys, are more durable and can withstand higher impact, making them ideal for heavy-duty milling operations. The choice between them depends on specific operational requirements.</p></p>
