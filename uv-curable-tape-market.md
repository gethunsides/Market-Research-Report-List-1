<p><ul><li>Single Side Tape Type</li><li>Double Side Tape Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV curable tape market is categorized into single-side tape and double-side tape types. Single-side tape features adhesive on one side, making it ideal for applications like labeling and surface protection. It adheres to surfaces without the need for additional bonding agents. In contrast, double-side tape contains adhesive on both sides, allowing it to bond two surfaces together securely. This type is commonly used in mounting, assembly, and packaging, offering versatility in various industries, including electronics and automotive.</p></p>
