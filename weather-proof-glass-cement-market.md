<p><p>Weather-proof glass cement is categorized into two main types: acid and neutral. Acidic glass cement contains substances that facilitate adhesion through an acid-based chemical reaction, making it effective for bonding glass in exposed environments. In contrast, neutral glass cement has a balanced pH, allowing it to adhere without causing damage to sensitive materials. This type is often preferred for applications requiring compatibility with diverse substrates, ensuring durability and resistance to harsh weather conditions while maintaining the integrity of the bonded materials.</p></p>
