<p><p>Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMMs) are essential tools in manufacturing and quality control, classified into two primary market types: CNC (Computer Numerical Control) and manually-controlled CMMs. CNC CMMs are automated systems that enhance precision and efficiency by using programmed movements for measurements, making them ideal for high-volume production. In contrast, manually-controlled CMMs require human operators to perform measurements, offering flexibility for low-volume or complex applications. Both types serve distinct purposes, catering to varying calibration, accuracy, and production needs.</p></p>
