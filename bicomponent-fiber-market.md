<p><p>The Bicomponent Fiber Market includes types like PE/PP, PE/PET, and Co-PET/PET, each offering unique properties. PE/PP fibers combine polyethylene and polypropylene, providing strength and elasticity, ideal for nonwovens and hygiene products. PE/PET fibers blend polyethylene with polyester, offering durability and softness, used in textiles and filtration. Co-PET/PET fibers feature a polyester core with a modified polyester sheath, enhancing thermal bonding and recyclability, suitable for high-performance applications like automotive and industrial fabrics. These fibers cater to diverse industries with tailored functionalities.</p></p>
