<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photomask Review System market includes two primary methods for assessing photomask quality: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB). The DD method compares individual die from different photomasks, ensuring consistency and defect-free manufacturing. In contrast, the DB method involves comparing die against a pre-established database of known acceptable defects, streamlining the quality control process. Both methods are essential for maintaining high standards in semiconductor manufacturing, minimizing defects, and ensuring optimal performance in integrated circuits.</p></p>
